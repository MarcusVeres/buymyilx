I am selling my 2013 Acura ILX (Dynamic Package). This is the last year Acura offered a manual transmission with the K24-Series engine.

The car drives and handles great. I've owned it since November 2012 and it's been an absolute pleasure! All repairs have been done by licensed mechanics and it has exclusively drank premium (93 octane) fuel.

I lived close to work (or worked from home) for 6 of the past 9 years, so it's mostly been used for grocery trips and weekend driving.

The car was given a safety inspection on July 2nd, 2021 and the CARPROOF is available here. Comes with both summer and winter wheel/ tire packages. Summer tires are currently installed.

Considering the age and mileage, I would say the car's in excellent condition. Typical wear and tear, blemishes and scratches for a vehicle this age are present. I replaced the battery and wipers in July to prepare it for sale.
